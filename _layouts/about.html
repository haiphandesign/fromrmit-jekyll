About

FromRMIT is a growing and living directory of young and bold RMIT University Vietnam students and alumni in Design and Media department or whoever originated from RMIT who is currently working in the design and media industry. It was created in an effort to increase the visibility of people from RMIT and a place for people to find, acknowledge and follow a new friend, a mentor or fresh, creative and talented individuals to join you on your team.

FromRMIT is also showcasing many of our best in a biweekly series where we interviewing people who originate from RMIT University Vietnam or featuring their creative works in Featured.


Nomination, Request an Edit or Opt Out

Head to our nomination page to nominate yourself, a friend in class or even someone that you knew originate from RMIT to help us build the directory. We usually review and update our directory database weekly on Saturday so if you just submit your nomination and haven't seen it on the directory, our team might not update it to the system yet.

To request a removal or an edit of your profile on our site, please send us an email at hello@fromrmit.com.
